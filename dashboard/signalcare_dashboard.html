Physiology and Habits (Individual Trends)

Heart Rate ?Nightly mean heart rate in beats per minute. Persistent upward shifts can reflect strain, arousal, or poor recovery.

RMSSD ?Nightly vagal HRV marker in milliseconds. Lower RMSSD over time can suggest reduced parasympathetic recovery.

LF/HF ?Sympathovagal balance proxy. Sustained increases can indicate sympathetic dominance; context matters across patients.

Sleep Hours ?Total sleep duration per night from aligned sleep records. Large reductions may precede worsening symptoms.

Light Avg ?Daily average ambient light exposure from wearable segments. Useful proxy for circadian rhythm regularity.

Calories (Daily Sum) ?Daily total calories burned, aggregated as sum of all sensor segments for the same day.
